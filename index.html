GAME RULES

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

- In each turn, a player rolls the dice as many times as he or she wishes. Each result gets added to the CURRENT score

- If the player rolls a 1, all CURRENT score points are lost and next player takes their turn

- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his CURRENT score gets added to his GLOBAL score, and the next player takes their turn

- The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
